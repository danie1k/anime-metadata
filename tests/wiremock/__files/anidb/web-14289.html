by Zytaku on 2019-08-21 23:09
This anime was overall good. The plot was simple but effective, the characters were diverse and likeable, and the protagonist, well, was generic, but still, better than most romcom empty, souless protagonists common in today's series. I skipped on watching it for a long time, deeming it a lesser series, but after seeing the above average reviews and reception, I decided to give it a try, and I enjoyed it.

Compared to the other anime about a guy tutoring a harem of girls, Go-Toubun no Hanayome, which focuses more on the harem aspect, this is the better series. Here, the characters are easier to like and relate, and unlike the quintuplets from Go-Toubun, they aren't all over the place, meaning that the personalities are more balanced instead of stereotypically clichéd. So in my opinion, if you are looking for a nice school harem series from 2019, I would recommend watching this over the other.

In short, a good series. Highly recommended.
